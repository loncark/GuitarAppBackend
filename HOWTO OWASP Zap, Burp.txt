OWASP Zap: just do an automatic scan on localhost, then

Burp Suite:
0. On entry: Temporary project, Burp defaults
1. Change to 'Proxy' tab.
2. In Firefox, go to Settings -> Network Settings ->
check Manual proxy config and click OK
3. In Burp, enable intercept.
4. Navigate to a page in Firefox, you shouldn't be able to
see it
5. The request will appear automatically in Burp, to forward
it to the browser, click 'Forward'
